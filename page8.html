HISTORY OF LEMONBALM



In the 10th century, lemon balm was introduced to Spain by the occupying Moors and was later brought to central Europe by Benedictine monks. In her book Physica, written between 1151 and 1158, Benedictine abbess Hildegard von Bingen (1098-1179) described medicinal uses of lemon balm.
