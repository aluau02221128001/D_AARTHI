HISTORY OF CALENDULA



Calendula flowers are sacred flowers in India and have been used to decorate the statues of Hindu deities since early times. The most common use in historic times was culinary, however, and the plant was used for both its color and its flavor. They were used for dumplings, wine, oatmeal and puddings.
