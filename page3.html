HISTORY OF LAVENDAR



In ancient Egypt it was used as a perfume and as an essential ingredient for incense. Lavender was a favorite ingredient in herbal baths of both Greeks and Romans. During the Middle ages it was considered an herb of love and was used as an aphrodisiac.
